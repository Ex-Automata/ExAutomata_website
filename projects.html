P.A.I.G.E Assistant

P.A.I.G.E Assistant is a cutting-edge modular desktop computer assistant designed to automate everyday tasks, empowering you to focus on what truly matters. As our flagship product, it offers a wide array of features, each available as standalone modules to ensure flexibility for your unique needs. Whether you're looking for seamless task management or enhanced productivity, P.A.I.G.E adapts to your workflow.

Compatible with leading AI platforms like ChatGPT, Bard, and more, P.A.I.G.E also caters to privacy-conscious users by supporting on-premise and on-device large language models. Its current key features include:

  • Automated time sheets
  • Procrastination warnings
  • Writing and coding assistant
  • AI-generated email drafts
  • Email management and summaries
Though still in the early stages of development, P.A.I.G.E is rapidly evolving with exciting plans for the future, such as:
  • Integration with common ticketing systems like Jira for task creation, management, and monitoring
  • Enhanced support for cross-team communication
  • Automatic spell checking
  • A smart file organizer for private and team databases
  • Advanced data analysis tools
With its modular design and expansive roadmap, P.A.I.G.E is more than just an assistant—it's a personalized productivity powerhouse tailored to your needs.

AI Book Club

Ever put down a book, only to forget key details when you pick it up months later? Struggled to recall who a reappearing character is after chapters of absence? Wondered about the deeper symbolism of a passage or the significance of a historical event in a non-fiction work? The AI Book Club is always in session to answer your questions and dive into meaningful discussions about any book—fiction or non-fiction.

Leveraging techniques like Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) and extensive meta-knowledge, the AI Book Club provides insights into storylines, characters, themes, symbolism, historical accuracy, and even complex concepts in academic or professional texts. Whether you're revisiting a favorite novel, exploring a dense scientific study, or unraveling historical intricacies, this tool is your ultimate companion for deeper understanding and thoughtful analysis.

Scribe Meeting Summarizer

Your hectic discussions, learnings, and decisions shouldn’t rely solely on memory once the meeting ends—especially when switching between different meeting platforms. The Scribe Meeting Summarizer is your ultimate tool for capturing and organizing meeting content. It works seamlessly across all major platforms, including face-to-face meetings, and delivers concise summaries along with actionable items that can be directly imported into your ticketing system.

Powered by state-of-the-art speech-to-text technologies like Whisper and speaker diarization, the tool ensures high accuracy in transcription and the ability to identify individual speakers. Moreover, it adapts to your needs by tailoring summaries based on user preferences, such as providing a legal perspective for lawyers or focusing on technical specifics for programmers.

Say goodbye to information overload and hello to clarity and productivity with a meeting summarizer that turns chaos into actionable insights.
