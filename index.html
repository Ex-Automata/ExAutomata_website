Paige Assistant (Coming Soon)

How much time per week do you waste on emails, documentation, reports, or copying data between tables? Paige changes that. A modular, privacy‑first desktop assistant (Windows, Linux, Mac) that proactively handles routine and complex tasks.
Enable only the modules you want, pick local, open‑source or cloud models per task, and choose a personality, voice, or avatar. Paige can integrate with emails, calendars, ticket and your data sources to automate workflows while you stay in control.

  • Desktop‑first & privacy‑respecting
  • Local, open‑source and cloud AI options, managed and BYO API Keys
  • Modular extensions with per‑task model choice
  • Proactive suggestions
  • Task routing and delegation across team members
  • Customizable personalities, voices, and avatars.
  • Role system - Tax advisor, Project Manager, Tech Lead, you decide!
Contact Talk to us

Philosophy

Paige is built for practical productivity: a single, extensible tool that adapts to people and organizations while keeping privacy first.

  • Reduce routine work — focus on what's imporant.
  • Proactive - chimes up when there're things to do.
  • One system instead of many subscriptions.
  • Modular, flexible, and configurable — useful for solo users and large teams alike
  • Privacy‑first by design — local and hosted options, transparent controls

NutriTracker (Early Alpha/Coming Soon)

AI webapp for logging nutrition and workouts with near‑zero friction. Track “roughly right” or meticulously: free‑text ("I ate sausages"), precise entries ("300 g, 400 kcal/100 g"), or image upload. Not a medical product.

  • Estimates calories, macros, micronutrients
  • Workout & health metric tracking
  • Connected nutrition database
  • Recommendations powered by AI

Philosophy

It’s better to track imperfectly than not at all. The AI fills in what you can't, but allows you to modify data as needed.

  • Quick capture - Near zero friction
  • Refine if you know better
  • AI suggestions
  • Privacy‑first defaults

Atlas — Pathfinder to your code (Work in progress)

Atlas is an work-in-progress open source VS Code extension that builds an interactive map of your workspace. It visualizes files, modules, and symbol relationships so you can trace connection paths and understand how the code of your project interacts, connects and changes, especially in regard to git commits and AI agents. Designed by developers for developers. Atlas helps reduce surprises, speedup onboarding, and surface likely impact before you commit.
